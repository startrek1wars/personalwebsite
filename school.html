School

I am in early high school. My high school is located at Earth, United States of America, in California. It is near Los Angeles. I attend six classes and am in the band. In this page you will find information about my school, classes, and other activities.


My High School

My high school opened on February 5, 1940 in order to relieve the students at Alhambra High. Originally, it only had one building-what is now called the "A" building. Later on, two new buildings-the "B" and "C" buildings. It now has a "D" building, but I am not aware of any other buildings in the school. It is a WASC (Western Association of Schools and Colleges Accrediting Commission for Schools) accredited public school, and recently underwent re-accreditation. In addition to academics, my high school offers sports such as football, tennis, and basketball.

Classes

My first class is Mandarin 1/2, where we (my classmates and I) learn how to speak, read, and write Mandarin.

My next class is Algebra II Honors, where Mrs. [CLASSIFIED] teaches us how to solve and graph equations.

We then have lunch, where we eat our lunches or go to club meetings.

After lunch, Mrs. [CLASSIFIED] teaches us about living things in Biology Honors.

Finally, Mrs. [CLASSIFIED] teaches us grammar in English 1/2 Honors.

Band

The only extracurricular activity that I participate in now is the high school band. It is a class during the day, and counts for P.E.-thus, I do not have to take P.E. In the band, we play woodwind, brass, and percussion instruments at events, such as football games. We also march in parades.
