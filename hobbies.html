Hobbies

As a student in early high school, I have almost as much free time as I did in early elementary school. This is because I only have a moderate amount of homework and studying to do. As a result, I have the time to read books, build models, and play video games. For as long as I can remember, my primary interests have been science fiction and aerospace. Read on for more information!


Star Trek

Star Trek has been my main interest ever since seventh grade. While I didn't watch many episodes, I discovered Star Trek books at my local library. After about three months of reading, I began to explore deeper in to the franchise. I watched episodes, played video games, and built models. I have become attached to all Star Trek from the Prime Universe-in other words, I am not as attached to the recent 2009 and 2013 Star Trek movies.

Star Trek Books

Reading Star Trek books is an activity that I do almost continuously, from the moment that I wake up to the moment that I fall asleep. In fact, my friends tell me that I ran into a pole while I was walking to class and reading a Star Trek book. However, I do not remember this accident. I follow many different series of books by multiple authors, but many of the characters in the books overlap. Also, most of the books take place in the 2370-2390 timeframe.

In reading these novels, I have become attached to many storylines and characters. This is one of the reasons why I will keep reading these novels even if a book or two of the series is of poor quality.

Star Trek: Online

When I started becoming interested in Star Trek, I began searching for a Star Trek video game to play. I finally found one in Star Trek: Online. Star Trek: Online is a video game developed by Cryptic Studios and owned by Perfect World Entertainment. It went live on February 2, 2010. I began playing the game when it went free-to-play in February of 2012. In the game, I play as a Starfleet captain who explores the galaxy on the ground and in space. In the latest update, Starfleet discovered a huge Dyson Sphere in the Delta Quadrant of the galaxy, inhabited by modified dinosaurs from Earth.


The game, now four years old, is a third-person MMORPG, meaning that it is a game that is meant to be played with other people. It also means that the game receives updates, called seasons, every couple of months. Thus, unlike normal games, where a gamer will play for twenty hours and then be finished, Star Trek: Online can be played for as long as the developers continue to create seasons. Star Trek Models

Another hobby of mine is to create Star Trek models out of paper. Some of these models I design and build myself. Others are designed on the internet. These I print out and build myself.

Building Star Trek models is an extremely satisfying endeavour. It is extremely cheap, because all I need is paper, color pencils, tape, and glue. It also results in a tangible representation of my time and effort. Plus, it's fun! However, each model requires much time and effort, and sometimes I stop working. In fact, in my room, I have many incomplete models that I never bothered to finish.

Aerospace

In addition to science-fiction flying machines, I also love real flying machines. When I was little, my father introduced me to airplanes. Nowadays, I love to earn about airplanes new and old. My favorites are the latest and greatest airplanes, such as the Boeing 787, an advanced passenger jet, and the Lockheed Martin F-35, an experimental fighter-bomber.

I love to read books about airplanes and autobiographies about pilots. I also like to build plastic paper models and fly airplanes in Flight Simulator X. However, I do these activities less often than, say, reading Star Trek books.
